First Aid Guide

Medical Needs & Precautions

First aid is the immediate assistance given to an injured or ill person before professional medical help arrives. Knowing basic first aid can save lives.

Guidelines & Remedies

Always stay calm and assess the situation before giving first aid. Use clean hands and tools to prevent infection.

Step-by-Step First Aid Instructions

CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

  • Check for responsiveness.
  • Call emergency services.
  • Start chest compressions.
  • Provide rescue breaths if trained.
